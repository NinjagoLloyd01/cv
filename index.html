Ninjago Season 15 is coming in June 2022!

The fifteenth season of Ninjago is preceded by Season 14 and The Virtues of Spinjitzu and succeeded by an unknown Ninjago 2023 installment. It will be released in 2022. A teaser for this season was released on the Portuguese channel SIC K on March 3rd, 2022, but was deleted soon after. The account associated with them on Instagram confirmed that this season will be premiering June 2022 in Portugal.
